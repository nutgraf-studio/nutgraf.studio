Methodology

How we work

Our process is a loop, not a line. Every piece of work is tested against its audience, and what we learn feeds the next pass.

  1. Research

    We frame the question before we touch the data.

  2. Collection

    We gather the evidence: field data, public records, scraped and reported sources.

  3. Cleaning

    We structure, check and verify until the data can carry weight.

  4. Visualisation

    We give the finding its clearest, most honest visual form.

  5. Interpretation

    We work out what the numbers actually support, and what they don't.

  6. Feedback

    We test the work with its audience. What we learn starts the next loop.

  7. back to Research
