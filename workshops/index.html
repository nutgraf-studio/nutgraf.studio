Flagship workshops

Teach your team to speak data fluently.

Flagship workshops in data research, data design and data storytelling, built around state-of-the-art AI, used well and used safely.

Book a workshop

State-of-the-art AI runs through every module.

Frontier models now find, draft and prototype faster than any team can alone. We teach you to use that speed, and to keep what matters human: verification, judgement, and control over your own data.

1

Data research

Find it, question it, trust it.

  1. Finding the numbers

    Use frontier AI to locate, extract and structure data from public sources, archives and your own documents in hours, not weeks.

  2. Interrogating the data

    Test for bias, gaps and uncertainty. Learn what your data can honestly claim, and where it stays silent.

  3. Verifying every claim

    Build fact-checking workflows that treat every AI output as a claim to check, never a fact to paste.

2

Data design

Give every finding its clearest form.

  1. Choosing the right form

    Chart, map, diagram or table: match each finding to the visual form that carries it best, before touching any tool.

  2. Prototyping with AI

    Sketch ten visual directions in the time one used to take, then apply human judgement to pick and refine the winner.

  3. Designing for the glance

    Hierarchy, colour and annotation that make the point land in seconds, on any screen and in print.

3

Data storytelling

Make it land, make it stick.

  1. Leading with the nutgraf

    Put the finding where nobody can miss it. Structure reports, decks and articles the way the best newsrooms do.

  2. Drafting with AI, editing like an editor

    Use language models for first drafts, summaries and translations, while voice, facts and nuance stay under your control.

  3. Presenting to the room

    Turn the analysis into briefings and talks calibrated to boards, funders, journalists or the public.

Length Half a day to two days
Where Your office, or remote
Group Six to twenty people
